Rachel Johnson

Hello! I am an undergraduate student at Notre Dame where I study physics and math. I am in the Glynn Family Honors Program and I am an officer for the Society of Physics Students. I am excited to learn new skills and right now I'm particularly interested in anything that brings physics and computer science together such as quantum computing or experimental particle physics. This past summer, I supported the GOES satellite team at Lockheed Martin as a Linux Systems Intern helping to prepare for the launch of GOES-T in early 2022. I am also the system administrator for The Observer.
